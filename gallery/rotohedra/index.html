Aldo Spizzichino's computer graphics

Visual experiments with rotogons and rotohedra

Aldo Spizzichino conceived this special solid of revolution and named them Rotogon and Rotohedron, depending whether the rotating figure is a polygon or a solid.

The image shown here was the only work he made on the subject, beside making this paper (in Italian) but inspired his son Eli to make a 3d visualization of them shown in this video-tutorial.
